Convour Supermarket: KPI Dashboard

Convour, is leading supermarket company that is facing a critical juncture in its operations. The company is keen on optimising its performance and gaining deeper insights into various facets of its business. AS a Business Analyst, I used Power Bi to analys the companies sales data and revealed key insights critical for the company's optimization and growth. Key findings include total revenue generated, quantity of products sold, average unit price, cost of goods sold, average customer rating, tax expenditure, profits generated, progress towards revenue goals, and comparative analysis of revenue, profit, and cost of goods.
